Bachelors Candidate in Computer Science Engineering
---------------------------------------------------

Matthew Scott Hernandez (He/Him) is a Mexican-American First Generation Bachelors Candidate in Computer Science Engineering at Michigan State University's College of Engineering. Matthew has been sharpening his skills in Python, JavaScript, Swift, and with Azure/Firebase Backend Solutions for the past six years. Matthew serves as a Digital Experience Intern at MSUIT where he focuses on remediating critical accessibility faults on core MSU.EDU websites. Earlier in the school year, he led one of the biggest projects in the MSU Informational Technology community in which he served as Lead Developer/Project Lead for a Virtual Assistant known as 'DCAT Spartan' which handles up to a hundred requests from internal faculty members daily. Matthew's solution has served the community well by significantly reducing the time it takes to access key essential utilities needed for web auditing/course content remediations.
